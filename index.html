About

Welcome! I am Joseph Olin, a software developer with a passion for mobile app development and software engineering in general.


I currently develop mobile apps at Voice of God Recordings alongside a crew of talented software developers. I actively work on many of VGR's Android apps, most noteably the Lifeline app, the Table app, and the apps on the Agapao Tablet. Without a doubt, what excites me the most about my job is the impact it has on people around the world. With six different languages in the Lifeline App and over 50 languages in the Table App, the mobile apps I work on at VGR have an international audience and a worldwide impact.


Prior to hiring on full time at Voice of God Recordings, I was a student at Indiana University Southeast, where I graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science. During one semester, I hired on as a software architecture intern at ZirMed, an up and coming tech company in Louisville, KY. While there, I worked on a server monitoring app with other interns, and began learning unit testing, architectural design, and many other software engineering concepts which are now oft-used pieces of my professional skillset.
