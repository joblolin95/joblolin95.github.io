About

I am a software developer with a passion for both software architecture and software engineering. I recently worked as an IT Intern at ZirMed, Inc, where I worked with a team of interns in the Architecture group to develop an internal database monitoring application. Through the development of this software, I learned an incredible amount concerning how to use different software libraries, tools, and design approaches. Currently, I work as a volunteer in the IT department of Voice of God Recordings, Inc.


Apart from that, I am also a student at Indiana University Southeast, majoring in Computer Science. Prior to attending college, I had never worked with code, let alone developed a piece of software. It was during my first programming class that I realized just how much I enjoy working with software and, since then, becoming a software developer has been my primary career goal. Following this plan, I will graduate in May 2018 with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science.
